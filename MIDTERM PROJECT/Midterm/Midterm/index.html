About the Product

This multi-purpose water bottle was designed for the everday person. Working citizen, athletes, and college students alike, this water bottle is for YOU. Why own so many different devices when you can own just one? The best part of it all is that this bottle is low maintenance and durable. You barely ever have to charge it because of its solar panel and it can withstand the pressures of water, heat and being dropped, making this bottle almost unstoppable! Have everything you could need while on the go with this water bottle.

Iphone Charger Compatible with Iphone 6,6S,6 Plus, 7, 7 Plus, 8, 8 Plus, and X. This built-in battery provides endless battery life for your phone with its charging and solar panel feature. Solar Panel Built in solar panel to provide continuous energy for the speaker and Iphone charger when you are out and about. Didn't have time to charge the bottle? No problem! This is an eco-friendly way to make sure you never run out of battery life no matter where you are!
Coffee/Tea Warming Function This water bottle has a warm beverage compartent! Never thought you would hear that before did you? Just press the warming button on the top to warm up your coffee, tea, etc. Within a couple minutes, the light on the button will turn from red to orange to green and you will have a nice warm cup of coffee waiting for you. This compartment can hold up to 12 oz. of any beverage.
Water Cooling/Purifying The water compartment is separated from the warm beverage compartment. With patented technology that allows you to put both hot and cold drinks in this one water bottle while also being able to keep them at their own respective temperatures! Who said you couldn't have the best of both worlds? It also has a purifier so you can fill the bottle up at a water fountain and still have that crisp taste. With activated fibers that extract heavy metals, bacteria, and chemicals from your water, no wonder why it tastes so pure. The filter is easily cleaned and can last for up to a couple of years under proper care. To cool the water, press the water drop button on the lid of the bottle and you can enjoy ice cold water in minutes! This compartment holds up to 12 oz. of water. Stay hydrated!
Waterproof With all of the electrically powered features of this water bottle, our designers decided it would be best to make the bottle waterproof. So if you accidently drop your bottle in a puddle or it gets rained on, it's not the end of the world; the speaker and charger will still be fully functional.
