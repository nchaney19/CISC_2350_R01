Out in the City?

Have you ever been out in the town and realized your phone is almost dead? Why invest in bulky portable phone chargers when your waterbottle has one built in? This lightweight bottle is something you can take any where and you can stay hydrated with some ice cold water while your phone is charging using our cooling function. Cool right? The battery life on this built-in phone charger can last anywhere from 6 extra hours of phone life once the bottle is charged (with our provided charger) or the battery life can extend far beyond that if the solar panel on the bottle has access to some sun. Do yourself a favor, stay connected, and keep yourself safe by always having your phone charged.
